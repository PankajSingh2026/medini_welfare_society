FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Medini Welfare Society is registered under Section 80G, so Indian taxpayers can claim a deduction. You'll receive a receipt by email after donating.

How is my donation used?

Funds go directly to active programs — learning centers, scholarships, health camps and relief work. Our annual report breaks down spending by program.

Can I volunteer if I don't live nearby?

Some roles, like donor outreach and content support, can be done remotely. Most direct program roles require being on-site. Apply on our volunteer page and we'll match you.

Do you partner with companies for CSR?

Yes — email contact@mediniwelfare.org with "CSR Partnership" in the subject line and our team will follow up with program options.
