About Us

Eleven years of working alongside the communities we serve.

Medini Welfare Society is a registered non-profit dedicated to social upliftment through education, health and livelihood programs.

Our Story

How Medini started

Medini Welfare Society was founded in 2014 by a small group of educators who noticed the same pattern across the villages where they taught: bright children losing access to learning simply because the support around them — tutoring, books, a quiet place to study — wasn't there.

What began as a single after-school room with twenty children has grown into a network of 12 learning centers, a scholarship program for girls, vocational courses for young adults, and health camps reaching thousands of children every year.

We still operate the way we started: close to the ground, working with local volunteers and partners, and measuring success by what changes in the families we work with.

Mission & Vision

What we're working toward

Mission — To widen access to education, health and livelihood opportunities for underserved communities through direct, accountable, community-rooted programs.

Vision — A future where a child's postcode no longer determines their access to a good education or a fair start in life.

Values — Transparency with donors, dignity for the people we serve, and decisions made with communities rather than for them.
