Volunteer with Medini Welfare Society.

We're always looking for tutors, healthcare volunteers, and people who can help with events and outreach.

How volunteers help

Most of our volunteers work directly in learning centers as tutors, support health camps as medical or logistics volunteers, or help coordinate events and donor outreach. We ask for a minimum 3-month commitment for tutoring roles so children have a consistent presence.

We provide orientation and basic training before any volunteer starts working directly with children or community members, including a background check for child-facing roles.
